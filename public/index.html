Welcome to the Stones Game!

Instructions:

Please select a player profile:

  • Player 1
  • Player 2
  1. Two players compete against each other.
  2. There are 3 piles of stones: Pile 1, Pile 2, and Pile 3.
  3. Players take turns picking up stones from a single pile.
  4. On each turn, a player can choose to pick 1 to 3 stones from the selected pile.
  5. The player who picks up the last stone loses the game.
  6. The game ends when all piles are empty.
  7. The first player to start is randomly determined.
  8. Enjoy the game and may the best player win!
